LTRC is one of India's foremost research centres in natural language processing, speech, machine translation, and information retrieval — bridging linguistic insight with computational innovation.

01
Foundational NLP Research Pushing the frontiers of language understanding, syntax, semantics, and discourse across Indian and world languages.
02
Inclusive Language Technology Building tools, datasets, and systems that serve linguistically diverse and historically underrepresented communities.
03
Education & Open Scholarship Training the next generation of researchers through rigorous coursework, mentorship, and open publication of resources.

Research Clusters

Our Labs unified view

NLP · Machine Translation

MT-NLP Lab

Syntax, parsing, semantics, WSD, discourse, treebanking, and machine translation. Computational models inspired by linguistics, combined with modern ML methods.

Explore Lab →
Classical · Translation

Anusaaraka Lab

Fusion of traditional Indian shastras and advanced modern technologies. A joint initiative with CIF and University of Hyderabad for Sanskrit-based MT.

Explore Lab →
Speech · Audio · Vision

Speech Processing Lab

Robust ASR, natural TTS, spoken information retrieval, and biometrics using speech and video. Goal-oriented basic research at the intersection of signal and language.

Explore Lab →
IR · IE · IA

IREL — Information Retrieval & Extraction Lab

Search, retrieval, and extraction for complex information needs. Fundamental research in Information Retrieval, Extraction, and Access for multilingual settings.
